What is the Chamber?

A massive steel structure with six pods surrounds the ring. Two superstars start, and the remaining four are released one by one at timed intervals. Elimination by pinfall or submission until only one stands.

Road to WrestleMania

Winners of both Chamber matches earn championship opportunities at WrestleMania 42 (April 18-19, Las Vegas). This is the last major stop before the grandest stage of them all.

The Elimination Chamber is a massive steel structure that surrounds the ring. Six competitors enter - two start in the ring while four are locked in individual glass pods. Every few minutes, a pod opens and another superstar enters. Wrestlers are eliminated by pinfall or submission. The last person standing wins.

This is one of the last major events before WrestleMania 42 (April 18-19 in Las Vegas). The Men's Chamber winner earns an Undisputed WWE Championship match, and the Women's Chamber winner earns a WWE Women's Championship match at WrestleMania. It shapes the biggest matches of the year.
